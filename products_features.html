Our Product Features

AI DETECTION

Campus Companion uses AI text recognition to automatically scan uploaded documents (like syllabi or assignment sheets). It detects important dates such as test days, project deadlines, and events without the user needing to input anything manually.

AUTOMATIC CALENDAR

Once deadlines are identified, the app syncs them directly to the user's chosen calendar platform (Google calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook).

CUSTOMIZABLE SCHEDULE MANAGMENT

Users can edit, prioritize, or categorize their events after import (e.g., Exams, Assignments, Clubs).
